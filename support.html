Getting Started

Who can use Konekta?

Konekta is an internal communication tool intended for employees and authorized personnel of Titan Collection and Credit Mgmt Inc. who are 18 years of age or older. If you need an account, contact your administrator or IT.

Which devices are supported?

Konekta is available across mobile and desktop. You can use the same account on all your devices.

DesktopWindows, Linux, and Mac (macOS)

Download Konekta from the App Store (iPhone, iPad, Mac) or Google Play Store (Android). Desktop builds for Windows, Linux, and macOS are distributed by your administrator or IT.

How do I sign in?

Open the app and sign in with the credentials provided to you (your name, phone number and/or email, and password). If you can't sign in, see Account & Sign-in below.

Messaging & Calls

Are my messages and calls private?

Yes. Your one-on-one and group messages and your voice and video calls are protected with end-to-end encryption. Their content is encrypted on your device and can only be read or heard by you and the people you communicate with. We cannot access the content of your encrypted messages or calls.

I'm not receiving message or call notifications.

  • Check that notifications are enabled: Settings → Notifications → Konekta (iOS) or System Settings → Notifications → Konekta (macOS).
  • Make sure Focus / Do Not Disturb is not silencing the app.
  • Confirm you have a stable internet connection (Wi-Fi or cellular).
  • Fully close and reopen the app, then send yourself a test message from another account.

My voice or video call has no sound or video.

  • Confirm Konekta has Microphone and Camera permission (see the table below).
  • Check that another app isn't already using the microphone or camera.
  • Make sure your device isn't muted and the volume is up.
  • If the problem continues, end the call, reopen the app, and try again.

A message failed to send.

Check your connection and try again. Messages are delivered once your device reconnects. If a message stays stuck, close and reopen the app.

Account & Sign-in

I forgot my password or can't sign in.

Contact your administrator or IT at it@titancollectioninc.com to reset your credentials. For security, we may verify your identity before making changes.

How do I update my profile?

Open the app's settings to update your display name, profile photo, or status.

How do I delete my account?

You can request deletion of your Konekta account by emailing it@titancollectioninc.com. We process deletion requests within 30 days. Deleting your account removes your profile and associated personal information from our systems, subject to legal and security retention requirements. See our Privacy Policy for details.

App Updates & Performance

How do I get the latest version?

Keep Konekta up to date through the App Store (iPhone, iPad, Mac) or Google Play Store (Android) so you always have the newest features and fixes. Enable automatic updates to stay current. For Windows, Linux, and macOS desktop builds, install updates provided by your administrator or IT.

The app is slow, crashing, or behaving unexpectedly.

  • Make sure you're on the latest version of Konekta and your operating system.
  • Fully close the app and reopen it.
  • Restart your device.
  • If the issue continues, email us with your device model, OS version, app version, and a description of what happened (screenshots help).

Privacy & Security

We take your privacy seriously. Messages and calls are end-to-end encrypted, data exchanged with our servers is encrypted in transit (TLS), and sensitive material such as encryption keys is stored securely on your device. For full details on what we collect and how we use it, read our Privacy Policy.
