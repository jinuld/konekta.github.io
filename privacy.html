Privacy Policy

Konekta · Last updated: June 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Titan Collection and Credit Mgmt Inc. ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use Konekta (the "App"), a secure messaging and calling application available on iOS and macOS. By using Konekta, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Privacy at the core. Your one-on-one and group messages and your voice and video calls are protected with end-to-end encryption. This means their content is encrypted on your device and can only be read by you and the people you communicate with. We cannot access, read, or listen to the content of your encrypted messages or calls.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Account information — such as your name (display name), phone number and/or email address, and authentication credentials, used to create and secure your account.
  • Profile information — such as a profile photo or status, if you choose to add one.
  • Content you share — messages, photos, videos, files, and (if you choose) your location shared within a chat. This content is end-to-end encrypted (see the callout above).
  • Support communications — information you provide when you contact us for help.

Information collected automatically

  • Device and technical information — such as device model, operating system version, and app version, used to operate and troubleshoot the App.
  • Push notification token — a device identifier provided by Apple Push Notification service and Firebase Cloud Messaging so we can deliver message and call notifications.
  • Connection metadata — limited information needed to route messages and calls and to keep your account secure.

What we do not do

  • We do not read the content of your end-to-end encrypted messages or calls.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use your data to track you across other companies' apps or websites for advertising.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App — delivering messages, enabling calls, and syncing your account.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account and communications secure.
  • To send notifications about new messages and incoming calls.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your requests.
  • To detect, prevent, and address abuse, fraud, and technical issues.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. How We Share Information

We share information only as needed to operate Konekta:

  • Service providers — we use trusted providers to deliver core functionality, including Apple (Push Notification service) and Google/Firebase (Firebase Cloud Messaging for notifications) and our own hosting infrastructure. These providers process data on our behalf and are bound by confidentiality and data-protection obligations.
  • Other users — content you send is delivered to the recipients you choose.
  • Legal reasons — we may disclose information if required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users and the public. Because messages and calls are end-to-end encrypted, we are technically unable to provide their content.
  • Business transfers — in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to this policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. Encrypted message content is stored only as needed to deliver it and is handled according to the App's design. When you delete your account, we delete or de-identify the personal information associated with it, except where we are required to retain it for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal information, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

Account deletion

You can request deletion of your Konekta account by contacting us at it@titancollectioninc.com. We process deletion requests within 30 days. Deleting your account removes your profile and associated personal information from our systems, subject to the retention exceptions above.

7. Security

We use end-to-end encryption for messages and calls, encryption in transit (TLS) for data exchanged with our servers, and secure storage on your device for sensitive material such as encryption keys. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your information using industry-standard safeguards.

8. Intended Users and Children's Privacy

Konekta is an internal communication tool intended for use by employees and authorized personnel of Titan Collection and Credit Mgmt Inc. who are 18 years of age or older. The App is not directed to, marketed to, or intended for use by children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own. Where required, we take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will revise the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, provide additional notice. Your continued use of Konekta after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
